Hi, I'm Sarah Moser

Front-end developer based in Tucson

I was a high school teacher for six years before becoming a front-end developer. Although there were aspects of teaching that I always enjoyed, I knew that it wasn't something that I could do all of my life. When I first set out to learn to code, I was overwhelmed at the task of having to teach myself correctly and hold myself accountable. I was relieved when I discovered SheCodes, because not only did SheCodes teach the material to me in digestible chunks and assign homework to check for understanding, but it linked me to a community of thousands of other women in the same boat as me. I feel as though SheCodes enabled me to follow through with my goal to change career paths, and created projects that now allow me to showcase my abilities and all that I've learned.
